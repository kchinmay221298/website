Privacy Policy

This website is primarily an informational website that directs visitors to third-party platforms such as a newsletter, a content hub, and social platforms. It is not designed to collect personal information directly through forms on this site.

1. Information collected on this website

This website itself is not intended to directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details through on-site forms.

If basic technical information is collected automatically by your hosting provider or analytics tools, that may include items such as browser type, device type, referring page, and general usage information.

2. Third-party links and platforms

This website contains links to third-party services, including external content and newsletter platforms. When you click those links, you leave this website and become subject to the privacy practices of those third parties.

  • Newsletter subscriptions may be handled on a separate platform.
  • Content hosted on external tools may have their own tracking or account systems.
  • Social and professional platforms may collect data according to their own policies.

3. Cookies and tracking

This website does not intentionally use a custom cookie system of its own within these pages. However, your browser, hosting provider, or third-party services you visit after clicking outbound links may use cookies or similar technologies.

4. How information may be used

If limited technical information is processed through hosting or analytics services, it may be used to keep the website functioning, understand basic traffic patterns, and improve the visitor experience.

5. Data sharing

This website is not intended to sell personal information. Any data handling performed by third-party providers would be governed by their own policies and systems.

6. Important note

This privacy policy is written to match the current structure of this website as a simple informational site with outbound links. If you later add forms, analytics tools, account systems, payment processing, downloads requiring sign-up, or other data collection features, this policy should be updated accordingly.
